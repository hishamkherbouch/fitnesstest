Fitness and Nutrition

Fitness:

To build an adequate workout routine I would recomend first addressing how many days a week you plan on exercising. If you are able to exercise 3 days a week follow a Full Body split, 4 days a week follow an Upper Lower split, 5 days a week follow a Push Pull Legs Upper Lower split, and 6 days a week follow a Push Pull Legs split.

Full Body Split
Day 1: Pick 1 chest exercise, 1 middle back exercise, 1 bicep exercise, 1 hamstring exercise and 1 abdominal exercise
Day 2: Pick 1 chest exercise, 1 middle back exercise, 1 tricep exercise, 1 quadricep exercise, and 1 calves exercise
Day 3: Pick 1 different chest exercise, 1 lats exercise, 1 glutes exercise, 1 forearm exercise and 1 traps exercise

Upper Lower Split
Day 1 & 3: Pick 1 chest exercise, 1 lats exercise, 1 middle back exercise, 1 biceps exercise, 1 triceps exercise and 1 traps exercise
Days 2 & 4: Pick 2 quadriceps exercise, 1 hamstring, glute, 1 calves and 1 of either an adductor or abductor exercise

Push Pull Legs Upper Lower Split
Day 1: Pick 2 chest exercises, 2 tricep exercises and 1 abdominals exercise
Day 2: Pick 2 middle back exercises, 1 lats exercise and 2 bicep exercises
Days 3 & 5: Pick 2 quadriceps exercise, 1 hamstring, 1 glute, 1 calves and 1 of either an adductor or abductor exercise
Day 4: Pick 1 chest exercise, 1 lats exercise, 1 middle back exercise, 1 biceps exercise, 1 triceps exercise and 1 traps exercise

Push Pull Legs
Days 1 & 4: Pick 2 chest exercises, 2 tricep exercises and 1 abdominals exercise
Days 2 & 5: Pick 2 middle back exercises, 1 lats exercise and 2 bicep exercises
Days 3 & 6: Pick 2 quadriceps exercise, 1 hamstring, glute, 1 calves and 1 of either an adductor or abductor exercise

Nutrition:

A healthy diet is essential for maintaining overall well-being and preventing various chronic diseases. It comprises a balanced intake of nutrient-rich foods that provide the body with essential vitamins, minerals, proteins, healthy fats, and carbohydrates. Incorporating a diverse range of whole foods such as fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, whole grains, and healthy fats like those found in nuts, seeds, and avocados is key. Such a diet offers numerous benefits, including improved immune function, enhanced energy levels, better weight management, and reduced risk of conditions such as heart disease, diabetes, and certain cancers. Additionally, consuming foods rich in antioxidants, fiber, and omega-3 fatty acids promotes brain health, supports digestion, and aids in maintaining healthy skin and hair. A balanced diet also includes staying hydrated by drinking an adequate amount of water throughout the day. Overall, adopting a healthy eating pattern not only nourishes the body but also fosters long-term vitality and quality of life.

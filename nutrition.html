Nutrition

General Tips For Nutrition

1. For optimal muscle growth and recovery you should consume 0.8-1g of protein per lb of body weight

2. Drink 0.5 fl oz of water per lb of body weight

3. To fuel workouts you should consume a meal high in protein a couple hours before your workout and another meal or snack high in fast digesting carbs an hour before working out.

4. After a workout you should consume a meal high in protein to help with recovery and muscle growth
